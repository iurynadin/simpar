Predictable and robust sustainable growth set the tone at SIMPAR in 2021

SIMPAR and its subsidiaries’ financial results for 2021 set new records. Consolidated net income hit R$ 1.3 billion, 155% more than in 2020 and Ebitda set a record of R$ 4.2 billion.


This performance reflects the meaningful investments made by SIMPAR in developing the subsidiaries and highly liquid assets.


Net Capex was an impressive R$ 8.8 billion (3.1x more than 2020) and a ROIC of 13.2%. Consolidated gross revenue was some BRL 15.5 billion (42% more than the previous year).
