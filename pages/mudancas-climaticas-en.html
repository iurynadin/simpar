We strive to make our business carbon neutral and drive innovation in the sector

Climate change initiatives and policies management is defined in our Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Emissions Program. In addition to measuring emissions from our subsidiaries’ operations, in line with emissions reduction plans debated in various global forums, measures are adopted that can mitigate impacts including:

  • the rational use of fuel
  • energy source innovation
  • ongoing renewal of the fleet
  • and monitoring indicators, in GHG Protocol inventories
  • offsetting and neutralization initiatives
